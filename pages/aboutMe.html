About Me

I was raised in South Dakota and obtained a bachelor's degree in Engineering Physics from the University of Oklahoma and a Masters degree from in Electrical Engineering from the Univeristy of Virginia. My career began at IntriCon, a medical device manufacturer in St Paul, MN. There I designed millimeter-scale electromagnetic coils for use in implantable medical devices. I also developed simulations mostly around the electrical performance of coils and data analytics software for driving manufacturing process improvements. One of my most proud moments was reverse engineering an old winding machine controller. and writing production data tracking software from scratch.
